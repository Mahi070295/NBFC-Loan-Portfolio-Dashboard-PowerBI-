# NBFC Loan Portfolio Dashboard — Power BI

## Overview
An executive-grade 3-page Power BI dashboard analysing the loan portfolio 
health of a Non-Banking Financial Company (NBFC) across branches, loan types, 
and financial years. Built to mirror how senior management consumes operational 
and risk reporting.

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Overview
- AUM growth trend (FY2022–2026) with forecast band
- Corporate liability & funding source mix
- Historical Net Interest Income vs Net Profit After Tax
- KPIs: Disbursement volume, Average ticket size, NII, NPAT

### 2. Risk & Asset Quality
- Gross NPA %: 19.95% | Net NPA: 14.52% | Provision Coverage: 31.85%
- Geographical concentration of credit risk by state
- Portfolio health ratio (Standard vs NPA)
- Branch Risk Diagnostic Ledger

### 3. Operational Performance
- Collection Efficiency %: 87.63%
- Annual billing volume vs cash recovery velocity
- Operational performance & field accountability ledger by branch
- Executive insight narratives identifying underperforming collection hubs

## Features
- Cross-page slicers: Financial Year, Loan Type, Branch
- Executive insight text on every page with actionable recommendations
- Branch-level drill-down for risk and collections

## Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop (DAX, Power Query, Data Modeling)
- Dataset: Simulated NBFC loan portfolio data
